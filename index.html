Common Small Birds in Mannheim (Excluding Corvids)

Quick Tips for Your Windowsill:

  • Tits: Crushed peanuts, sunflower hearts (chipped), dried mealworms, and insect suet
  • Robin/Wren: Dried mealworms and insect suet on a small tray (soft-food specialists)
  • Finches: Goldfinches need Nyjer in fine-port tubes; greenfinch/chaffinch prefer hearts/safflower
  • Low-residue options: Dried mealworms, safflower, insect-suet nuggets, Nyjer tubes
